What is Noob Defence Simulator?

  • Noob Defense Simulator is an exhilarating tower defense game on Roblox where your strategic skills are put to the test. In this action-packed game, you must defend your base from relentless waves of noobs by strategically placing and upgrading various towers. Each type of tower offers unique abilities to help you fend off different kinds of noob attackers, from speedy runners to formidable bosses. Earn in-game currency and experience points by defeating noobs, which you can use to enhance your defenses. With both solo and multiplayer modes, Noob Defense Simulator provides endless fun and challenges for players looking to showcase their tactical prowess and teamwork.
